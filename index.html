You are Sayf...

I often wished for an easy way to tell my support crew how I was doing, without having to actually say much. This is where I got the idea for SAYF, a mobile app that will, at the push of a button, say “I'm safe”, “I need help”, or “please check in on me”.

SAYF will say what you can't.

The app will be intuitive, calming, and beautifully designed, but most importantly it will offer an easy solution for people experiencing mental health crisis to reach out for support. While it’s a simple idea, I believe it has the power to save lives – or at least bring help or comfort when needed.
